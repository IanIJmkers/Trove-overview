Trove

Your TCG collection tracker with real-time market data, AI grading, and set completion tracking.

01 — Portfolio

Track your collection's real-time value

Add your cards and instantly see their market value powered by active eBay listings. Use the AI Grading tool to estimate what grade your card would receive, and the Centering tool to check alignment before sending cards off for grading.

  • Live market value from eBay
  • AI-powered grade estimation
  • Centering analysis tool
  • Gains & losses at a glance
02 — Chase

Hunt smarter at every convention

Build wishlists of cards you're hunting at your next convention. Use the built-in calculator with percentage-based modifiers to instantly evaluate deals on the spot and never overpay again.

  • Convention-ready wishlists
  • Deal calculator with % modifiers
  • Per-card value adjustments
  • Real-time price reference
03 — Sets

Track your master set journey

Visual progress bars show exactly how close you are to completing each set across Pokemon and One Piece TCG. Check off cards as you collect them and watch your progress grow.

  • Pokemon & One Piece TCG sets
  • Visual completion progress
  • Card-by-card check-off
  • Grid & list views
04 — Market

Instant market intelligence

Search any card to see its current market value from live eBay listings. Filter by grading company and grade, or use the PSA slab scanner to instantly identify and price a graded card by scanning its barcode.

  • Live eBay pricing data
  • PSA / BGS / CGC filters
  • PSA barcode slab scanner
  • Direct purchase links
05 — Profile

Your account, your way

Manage your account with Apple and Google sign-in, toggle between USD and GBP, and report bugs directly to the team. Simple and secure.

  • Apple & Google sign-in
  • Currency toggle (USD / GBP)
